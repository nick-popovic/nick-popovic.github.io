About Us

Alset Consulting Ltd. is a privately owned Canadian technology company that provides software and consulting services mostly, but not limited to financial and brokerage industry since incorporation in 1995.

We specialize in solutions for accessing all Canadian real time market data and NBBO (National Best Bid & Offer) feeds, DMA (Direct Market Access), consolidated fills and orders drop copies, various broker/dealer generic and customized reports, trader workstations and data visualization.

Our solutions are primarily based (but not limited to) on the following technologies: C/C++/Java/Python in Windows/Linux/Unix environments, TCP/IP/Multicast, client/server, low latency/high throughput messaging, scriptig, GUI, Data Extraction/Harvesting/Visualization, RDBMS/SQL etc.
